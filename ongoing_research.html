Ongoing Research at Bio-Positive Lab

We are a team of researchers passionate about developing sustainable solutions for the future. Our current research focuses on several exciting areas:

Area of Interests

  • Microbial Electrochemical Technologies: We investigate the use of microbes in bioelectrochemical systems for renewable energy production and wastewater treatment. This includes studying biofilm formation and mitigation strategies to optimize these systems.
  • Biohythane Production: We explore the biological conversion of organic matter into biohythane, a clean-burning fuel composed of hydrogen and methane. This research focuses on developing efficient and cost-effective methods for biohythane production.
  • Nanomaterials for Bioenergy Applications: We design and characterize novel nanomaterials for improved efficiency in bioenergy production processes. These nanomaterials can be used to enhance catalysts, improve mass transfer, or develop new types of bioreactors.
  • Microalgal Biomass Production: We investigate the cultivation of microalgae for biofuel production and other applications. This research focuses on optimizing growth conditions, developing harvesting techniques, and exploring the potential of microalgae for bioremediation.
  • Sustainable Bioenergy and Biofuels: Our research aims to develop innovative bioenergy and biofuel technologies that are environmentally friendly, efficient, and scalable. We explore various approaches to achieve a sustainable bioeconomy.
